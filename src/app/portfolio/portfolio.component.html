Hearthstone is an online card game which I have a love/hate (mostly hate) relationship with. People describe it as a cross between poker and chess, but I'm quite bitter about it and think it's mostly a random number generator in card form.

From Wikipedia - The game is a turn-based card game between two opponents, using constructed decks of 30 cards along with a selected hero with a unique power. Players use their limited mana crystals to play abilities or summon minions to attack the opponent, with the goal of reducing the opponent's health to zero.
